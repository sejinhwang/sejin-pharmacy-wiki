# Meta-Database

Cross-database topic index used to connect related pharmacy topics across practice areas, certification domains, and specialty collections.

## Intended Use

- Maintain canonical cross-links between related topics.
- Track overlapping learning objectives across databases.
- Support future migration of Notion relations into wiki backlinks.

## Position in the Wiki

The Meta-Database sits above the three main content areas: Pharmacy Practice, BPS, and Other Certifications.
