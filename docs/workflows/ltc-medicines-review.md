# LTC Medicines Review

## Purpose

Structured long-term care medicines review for admission, quarterly review, annual review, and clinically triggered review.

## Review domains

- Goals of care and frailty context
- Indication and ongoing need
- Effectiveness
- Safety and tolerability
- Renal function and dose appropriateness
- Falls, delirium, cognition, sedation, anticholinergic burden
- Administration feasibility
- Monitoring plan

## Documentation approach

Use POMR-based problem list updates and clearly distinguish active medicines-related problems from resolved or monitored issues.
