# Discharge MedRec

## Purpose

Support safe discharge medicines reconciliation, TTO preparation, patient counselling, and transition-of-care communication.

## Checklist

- Compare inpatient active medicines against BPMH and discharge plan
- Identify continued, stopped, held, changed, and newly started medicines
- Confirm indication, dose, route, frequency, duration, and monitoring
- Identify reimbursement / Special Authorisation issues where applicable
- Prepare counselling points and follow-up recommendations

## Output

Use concise, copy-paste-ready notes for Epic / Connect Care discharge workflow.
