# AKI / CKD Medicines Review

## Purpose

Pharmacist review of medicines in AKI, CKD, or rising creatinine.

## Review checklist

- Current Cr/eGFR trend and baseline renal function
- Volume status and haemodynamic context
- Nephrotoxic medicines
- Renally cleared medicines requiring dose adjustment
- Medicines to hold temporarily during AKI
- Electrolyte risks, especially K and bicarbonate issues
- Monitoring and restart plan

## Common medicine groups

- ACE inhibitor / angiotensin II receptor blocker
- Diuretics
- Metformin / Glucophage
- SGLT2 inhibitors
- DOACs
- Gabapentinoids
- Antimicrobials
- NSAIDs
