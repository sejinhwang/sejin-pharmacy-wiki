# Alberta Pharmacy Care Plan

Source: [Notion page](https://www.notion.so/2ebeca3ee5b18054b78dd06f78f719f4)

## Eligibility Criteria

### Comprehensive Annual Care Plan (CACP)

- [ ] Two chronic diseases from [Chronic Conditions | Column A](#chronic-conditions-column-a)
- [ ] One chronic disease from [Chronic Conditions | Column A](#chronic-conditions-column-a)
- [ ] Plus one risk factor from [Risk Factors | Column B](#risk-factors-column-b)

### Standard Medication Management Assessment (SMMA)

- [ ] One chronic disease from [Chronic Conditions | Column A](#chronic-conditions-column-a)
- [ ] Taking **3 or more** different Schedule 1 medicines

### Diabetes SMMA

- [ ] Diabetes mellitus
- [ ] Taking insulin, or 1 or more Schedule 1 medicines
- [ ] Not previously received a CACP or SMMA
- [ ] Diabetes SMMA cannot be claimed if CACP/SMMA already claimed

### Tobacco Cessation SMMA

- [ ] Daily tobacco use
- [ ] Willing to receive tobacco cessation counselling and support, including pharmacotherapy
- [ ] Maximum of **4** Tobacco Cessation follow-ups per 365 days
- [ ] May be claimed in addition to CACP, SMMA, or Diabetes SMMA
