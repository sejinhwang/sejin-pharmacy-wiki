# Nutrition Support and Enteral Tubes

Source: [Notion page](https://www.notion.so/fade46fc56ee4a3db426fc99baf6345f)

## Learning Objectives

- Discuss pharmacist and interdisciplinary roles in parenteral nutrition and enteral feeding.
- Describe place in therapy for parenteral nutrition and enteral feeding.
- State main components of parenteral nutrition and enteral feeds.
- Define refeeding syndrome and a management approach.
- Describe administration of oral medicines via feeding tubes.
- Describe management of drug-nutrient interactions.

## Core Sections

- Interdisciplinary roles in nutrition support
- Enteral nutrition versus parenteral nutrition
- PN and enteral feed components
- Refeeding syndrome
- Medicines via feeding tubes
- Drug-nutrient interactions
- Formula stability and transitions of care
