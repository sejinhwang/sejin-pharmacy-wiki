# Hospital Pharmacy Administration and Management

Source: [Notion page](https://www.notion.so/069833f7a3d446f5ace068cbfea0443e)

## Learning Objectives

- Describe hospital-based medicine policy and who manages it.
- Describe key constituents of the regulatory framework for hospitals and health systems.
- Describe the role of ISMP Canada and hospital medication-safety principles.
- Describe interdisciplinary team roles alongside pharmacists.
- Describe clinical pharmacy key performance indicators.
- Compare pharmacy team management models in hospitals.
- Explain the difference between pharmacy management and pharmacy leadership.

- Hospital medicine policy governance
