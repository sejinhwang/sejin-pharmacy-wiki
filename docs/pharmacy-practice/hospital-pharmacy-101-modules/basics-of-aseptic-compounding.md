## Learning Objectives

- Define aseptic compounding and distinguish it from non-aseptic compounding.
- Explain hazardous versus non-hazardous aseptic compounding.
- List reasons aseptic compounding matters in hospital practice.

- Identify common aseptically compounded products.
- Describe standards and quality-control principles.
