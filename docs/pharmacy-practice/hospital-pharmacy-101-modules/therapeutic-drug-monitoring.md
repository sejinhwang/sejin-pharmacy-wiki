# Therapeutic Drug Monitoring

Source: [Notion page](https://www.notion.so/c2cd168bd90841d6a4c9acc5d1abd955)

## Learning Objectives

- Describe the importance of therapeutic drug monitoring in hospital pharmacy practice.
- Identify when therapeutic drug monitoring is warranted.
- Discuss the hospital pharmacist role in therapeutic drug monitoring.
- Perform a systematic therapeutic drug monitoring consult.
- Apply therapeutic drug monitoring to vancomycin, tobramycin, and lithium.

- Indications and importance of TDM
- Pharmacist role in TDM
- Systematic TDM consult workflow
- Vancomycin AUC-guided monitoring
- Tobramycin monitoring
- Lithium monitoring
