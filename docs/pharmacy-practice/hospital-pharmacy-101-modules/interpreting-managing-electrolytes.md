# Interpreting and Managing Electrolytes

Source: [Notion page](https://www.notion.so/75ab74e08379409a9d2ff7eb50a91a64)

## Learning Objectives

- Identify common inpatient electrolyte disorders and common causes.
- Name oral and IV therapies used to manage common electrolyte disorders.
- Explain why electrolyte disorders require treatment.
- Describe a structured approach to identify and treat electrolyte disorders.
- Discuss practical treatment considerations.
- Develop monitoring plans for electrolyte disorders.

## Core Sections

- Why treating electrolyte disorders matters
- Immediate safety screen
- Mechanism-based interpretation
- Sodium, potassium, magnesium, phosphate, and calcium disorders
- Practical treatment considerations
- Monitoring bundles
